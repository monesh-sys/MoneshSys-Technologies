Team Rules & Code of Conduct

MoneshSys Technologies

These team conversation rules are created to maintain a professional, respectful, and productive environment at MoneshSys Technologies.

1. Professional Communication

  • Be respectful and polite to all team members
  • No abusive, offensive, or inappropriate language
  • Treat everyone equally and fairly

2. Work-Focused Communication

  • Team groups are strictly for project-related discussions
  • Avoid spam, jokes, or unrelated messages
  • Use private chat for personal matters

3. Clear Messaging

  • Write clear and meaningful messages
  • Provide context before asking questions
  • Avoid unnecessary tagging

4. Availability & Updates

  • Inform the team if you are unavailable
  • Share regular task updates
  • Respond within a reasonable time

5. Task & Decision Policy

  • Do not make major changes without approval
  • Follow assigned tasks and deadlines
  • Final decisions are taken by the Founder

6. Privacy & Confidentiality

  • Do not share internal files or ideas outside the team
  • Respect personal and company privacy
  • No misuse of company resources

7. Feedback & Issues

  • Give feedback respectfully
  • Raise issues privately with the Founder
  • Avoid arguments in public group chats

8. Original Work Policy

  • All work must be original
  • No plagiarism or copying
  • Proper credit will be given for contributions

9. Rule Violations

  • First violation → Warning
  • Repeated violations → Removal from the team

By joining and participating in MoneshSys Technologies, you agree to follow these team rules and maintain professionalism at all times.
